My proposal topic is user experience in video games. As a senior at the School of Information on the UX design pathway, much of my coursework and expertise is related to understanding the wants and needs of the user/client. From my understanding, video games are designed to keep players engaged from the gameplay to the music. This is why I believe exploring the user experience in games would be worthwhile not just for understanding the fun side of games but also their educational potential.
